# PACER Chapter 13 Discharge Research Guide

## Prerequisites
- Active PACER account (https://pacer.uscourts.gov)
- Sufficient account balance for searches

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Search Method

### Option A: Known Court (Specific District)
Best if you know which bankruptcy court(s) to search.

1. Go to https://pacer.uscourts.gov
2. Click "Log in to..." → "PACER Log In"
3. Select the bankruptcy court from the dropdown
4. Click "Query" on the left menu

### Option B: Nationwide Search (PACER Case Locator)
Best for searching multiple courts at once.

1. Go to https://pacer.uscourts.gov  
2. Click "Log in to..." → "PACER Case Locator (PCL)"
3. Select "Bankruptcy" as the case type

---

## Step 2: Build Your Search Query

### For Chapter 13 Cases Only:
- **Case Code:** 13 (Chapter 13)
- **Case Type:** bk (bankruptcy)

### Date Filtering:
Look for cases where:
- **Date Closed:** Last 7 days (discharge typically closes the case)
- **OR Date Last Filed:** Recent activity

### Example Search Fields:
```
Case Code: 13
Date Closed: [Start Date] to [End Date]
Court: [Select specific district or "All Bankruptcy Courts" in PCL]
```

---

## Step 3: Execute the Search

1. Fill in your search criteria
2. Click "Run Query"
3. **Cost Alert:** You'll be charged $0.10 per page of results (1 page ≈ 54 lines)

---

## Step 4: Review Search Results

The results table shows:
- Case number
- Case title (debtor name)
- Court
- Date filed
- Date closed
- Case status

**Cost:** Results page is billable (typically 1-3 pages for small date ranges).

---

## Step 5: Access the Docket Report

1. Click on the **case number** link in the results
2. This opens the Docket Report
3. **Cost:** Docket reports are capped at $3.00 (30 pages max)

### What to Look For:
- Docket entry for **"Order Discharging Debtor"** or **"Discharge of Debtor"**
- Date of discharge
- Any **Form 423 (Notice to Debtor)** entries

---

## Step 6: Access Case Information for Address

From the docket:
1. Look for **"Voluntary Petition"** (first entry)
2. Click document link to view PDF
3. **Cost:** PDFs billed at $0.10 per actual page, capped at $3.00
4. **Address is on:** Page 1 of the petition (debtor's address)

### Alternative:
1. Click **"Party"** or **"Attorney"** tabs
2. View debtor information (name, address, SSN last-4)
3. **Cost:** Usually 1 page ($0.10)

---

## Step 7: Download/Document

- PDFs can be downloaded and saved locally
- Right-click → "Save As" or use PACER's download button

---

## Cost Summary (Typical Search)

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Search query | $0.10-$0.30 |
| Docket report | Up to $3.00 |
| Petition PDF (address) | $0.10-$3.00 |
| **Total per case** | **$0.30-$6.40** |

**Note:** If your quarterly charges are under $30, PACER waives the fees.

---

## Tips for Efficiency

1. **Narrow date ranges** = fewer search result pages
2. **Specific courts** = faster than nationwide PCL
3. **Use Case Locator for broad searches**, individual court for targeted searches
4. **Save PDFs locally** — re-accessing costs money again

---

## Important Reminders

- PACER data is public record
- Each click/page load may incur charges
- Transaction receipts available in "Manage My Account" → "Usage"
- Review PACER's acceptable use policy at pacer.uscourts.gov/policy-procedures

---

## Alternative: PACER Case Locator Advanced Search

For more complex queries:
1. Log into PCL
2. Click "Advanced Search"
3. Combine filters:
   - Case type: Bankruptcy
   - Chapter: 13
   - Date range: Last 7 days
   - Court: Select specific districts

This searches multiple courts simultaneously but may return more results (higher cost).
