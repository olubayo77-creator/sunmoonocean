# Key Principles Summary

## For AI Assistant (MICHAEL)
1. **Always be honest about being AI** — builds trust, reduces creepiness
2. **Build in 5-10 second pauses** — marked as `[pause: Xs]` in script
3. **Lead with empathy, not facts** — acknowledge stress/vulnerability first
4. **Ask permission to continue** — "Want to hear more?" vs assuming
5. **Direct rate/pressure questions to human** — don't overstep
6. **Validate concerns** — "You're right to ask" / "That makes sense"

## For Licensed Broker (DANIEL)
1. **Warm handoff** — acknowledge previous conversation
2. **Lead with reassurance** — "We're going to give this everything"
3. **Explain the WHY** — not just "we need this" but "this protects you"
4. **Address rate concerns proactively** — be upfront about 13%
5. **Clear next steps** — numbered lists, specific timelines
6. **Urgency without panic** — "We can do this" vs "You're running out of time"

## Universal Rules
- **Never:type instant replies** — even 3 seconds feels more human
- **Use shorter sentences** with breaks — easier to digest when stressed
- **Acknowledge emotion before responding** — "I hear you" / "That's scary"
- **Type out uncertainty** — "Let me check on that" vs instant perfect answers
- **End with questions** — keeps conversation moving naturally
